# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all participants to be respectful, professional, and collaborative.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Conducting research and testing responsibly and ethically

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments or conduct related to protected characteristics
- Deliberate disruption of discussions
- Unsafe or unethical research practices
- Unauthorized testing on production systems

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and taking appropriate corrective action in response to instances of unacceptable behavior.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the project, including the repository, issue tracker, and any related communications channels.
